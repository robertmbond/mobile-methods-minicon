What are mobile methods?

Mobile methods examine how people use mobile technologies—especially smartphones—and how digital traces from everyday use can be studied responsibly. The Mini-con highlights both substantive research on mobile communication and methodological innovation in using mobile-generated data for scholarship.

The event is designed to support Communication scholarship while connecting with allied social science fields interested in behavior, media, and social life in mobile environments.
